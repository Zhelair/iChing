# Yi Path — Monetization

## Principle

Pay for depth, convenience, and carefully produced content—never for access to the oracle itself.

## Proposed model

- **Free forever:** all casting methods, all 64 basics, source view, unlimited local journal, and JSON backup.
- **Three free AI reflections:** enough for someone to understand the optional feature before paying.
- **Yi Path Plus:** roughly €3.99/month or €29/year—AI reflection, a short follow-up conversation, encrypted optional sync, advanced journal search and pattern links, and polished exports.
- **One-off packs:** an expert history course, yarrow workshop, licensed translations or commentaries, and audio journeys.
- **Support Yi Path:** a quiet voluntary support link in Settings.
- **No advertisements:** never place advertising in or around a reading.

## Current-session boundary

AI features, accounts, sync, subscriptions, and payments are product plans only. They are not part of the current implementation session.
